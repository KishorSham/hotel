Bungalow Suites

Our Florida Keys suites, nestled in bungalows, are so peaceful it's like living alone in the lap of luxury.

"Set on a private five acre islet and surrounded by sherbet-green seas, Little Palm intelligently integrates eye-popping ecology with stylish suites, inventive cuisine and a priceless shield of privacy."  - Forbes Traveler

Our thirty Florida Keys suites, nestled two apiece in thatched-roof bungalows, are so silent and peaceful, it’s like you’re living alone in the lap of luxury.  Island Escape Suite, Island Elegance Suite, Island Romance Suite and Island Grand Suite guest quarters are housed in thatched roof bungalows, with custom-made king-sized beds with romantic butterfly netting, so plush some guests never want to leave.

Accessible Suites

Little Palm Island offers two accessible suites. In addition to all or most features of the corresponding room type, these rooms may include:

  • Ramp leading to suite
  • Wheelchair threshold
  • Roll-in showers
  • Seat in the inside and outside showers
  • Handheld shower in both inside and outside showers
  • Railing in the inside shower
  • ADA compliant toilet with bars on one side and back, flush handles on user side
  • Knee protection on sinks in vanity
  • Telephone has big buttons
  • Lamps have pull chains
  • High lift toilet seat available
  • Kit with door knocker, door bell with strobe, bed shaker alarm clock, telephone signaler, telephone amplifier, Portaview telephone keyboard / word viewer
