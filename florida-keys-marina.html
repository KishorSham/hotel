Marina

Learn about our Florida Keys Marina. Dry land has never looked so inviting.

If you're sailing or yachting through Florida Keys, Little Palm Island marina is the ideal place to dock for a few days.

Is-land Ahoy!

A friendly beacon to seafarers, you’ll enjoy world-class cuisine at the resort's dining room, body treatments for the ocean-weary at SpaTerre, beach for stretching out in the sun, and postcard-perfect island beauty.

Informations & Reservations:
Contact our Dockmaster
dockmaster@littlepalmisland.com
305-872-2524

Facilities

Our Florida keys marina slips will accommodate vessels up to 120 feet in length with a draft up to six feet, in addition to 575 feet of dock space bordering a protected lagoon, ideal for smaller boats. You will have 50 or 30 amp electricity service, a water allowance of one gallon per foot each day, and full use of the island's recreational facilities, including pool, snorkeling and fishing gear. You may also enjoy the superlative offerings of the resort's dining room and spa.

Directions

Little Palm Island in the Florida Keys can be found at:

Latitude     24°36.78
Longitude  81°23 .63

Just 4 nautical miles north of Looe Key Light (marker 24).
The Loran coordinates are 13981.6 and 43530.5 at Flashing Red #2, which marks the entrance to Newfound Harbor.
