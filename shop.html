Shop Little Palm Island

Unique gifts, apparel and artwork fill our tropical stores.

Our Island and Welcome Station Gift Shops excite the curious shopper. Unique gifts, apparel and artwork fill our tropical stores. Items from around the world are included with a variety of pieces from local artisans. For those who want a reminder of their stay on the island, or for those who dream of the day they will visit, we offer a wide array of logo items. These items can be ordered directly from our gift shop. Simply indicate which items you would like and our staff will contact you and ship your choices directly from paradise!

How to Order Logo Items

Our Boutiques are located at our Shore Station and on our Island. Unique gifts from classic to contemporary to please the most discerning of tastes.
